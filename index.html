About

Reach NC Voices is an initiative of EducationNC working to bring unheard voices back to the public discourse. Through direct conversation, we want to show people they matter, so they will be empowered to civically engage. Too many North Carolinians are not having their stories heard. And now we want to use our technology to listen.

  • It Starts with a Question

    Maybe it is about how much principals should be paid or how people are being affected by housing costs.

  • We take that question to the community.

    We meet you where you are — inbox, phone, or in your community — and invite you to share your ideas.

  • Analyze the responses and take action

    This might mean writing a story, sending a report to a policymaker, or fostering continued conversation.

  • Then — always — return to you.

    Having a conversation with Reach means we will always provide you with helpful information.
